About Us

Helping hand foundation is a crowdfunding organisation that aspires to tell the stories of all thise around the world that need help. We focus on making all voices heard and seen. In these trying times, many of us need help and need a platform to reach out on. Our organisation hopes to connect the world and spread the message of love and kidness through donations.
